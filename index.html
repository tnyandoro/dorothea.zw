About Us

Our Belief

We believe that God called the Dorothea Mission into being for the purpose of evangelism - primarily in the urban townships, but also in the rural areas and on farms. Our call is to lead men, women and children out of darkness into a deep relationship with Jesus Christ.
The Headquarters of the Dorothea Mission is in South Africa and at present we have branches in Zambia, Malawi, Mozambique, Zimbabwe.

Evalengical work

"Preach the word; be prepared in season and out of season; correct, rebuke and encourage--with great patience and careful instruction." 2 Timothy 4:2
In Christianity, evangelism is the commitment to or act of publicly preaching (ministry) of the Gospel with the intention to share the message and teachings of Jesus Christ.

Bread of Life

"Then Jesus declared, “I am the bread of life. Whoever comes to me will never go hungry, and whoever believes in me will never be thirsty." John 6:35.
Most of our evangelists are trained at the Dorothea Bible College near Rosslyn (North West of Pretoria) where a three-year course is offered..

Hospital Ministry

"I was sick and you visited me." Matthew 25:36.
Hospital Ministry is committed to ministering the love and compassion of Jesus Christ through personal hospital visits for you and your loved ones
